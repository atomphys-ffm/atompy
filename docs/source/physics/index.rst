=================
Physics Submodule
=================

The :code:`pyhsics` submodule offers some theoretical modelling of various
atomic and molecular physics.

The module :doc:`physics` is about general atomic and 
molecular physics.

The module :doc:`compton_scattering/index` is about Compton
scattering in particular.
