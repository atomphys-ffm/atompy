=======
Physics
=======

The :code:`pyhsics` submodule offers some theoretical modelling of various
atomic and molecular physics.

The module :ref:`atompy.physics` is about general atomic and 
molecular physics.

The module :ref:`atompy.physics.compton_scattering` is about Compton
scattering in particular.
